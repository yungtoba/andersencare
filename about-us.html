About Us

Who We Are

A trusted provider of flexible temporary and permanent staff to the care industry in London and the Southeast.

Our Story

Andersen Care

Andersen Care is a trusted provider of flexible temporary and permanent staff to the care industry in the London and southeast areas.

Andersen Care are involved in the provision of personal care and support for those individuals who cannot wholly look after themselves completely without assistance regardless of age, sex, race, cultural or religious beliefs.

We provide our service either within a care home environment or in the comfort of your own home, at your convenience and in ways you will find most agreeable.

Our Approach

How We Deliver Care

Central to our belief in Andersen Care, are the care needs requirements of service users and their family members.

Individual Package of Care

We aim to provide each service user with a tailored care package aligned with their personal and health needs.

Multi-Agency Cooperation

We collaborate with local authorities and professionals to maximise independence and support outcomes.

Thorough Assessment

We conduct detailed assessments from referral through care planning to ensure accurate support delivery.

Flexible Response

Our care adapts continuously to the changing needs of each service user.

Community Focus

Local Recruitment

Andersen Care undertakes to recruit its workers from within the local community to be serviced by a contract. We believe that by recruiting local Care Staff, we are able to maximise our retention levels.

This reduces the amount of time spent on travel, thus providing maximum capacity of service to our Clients.

Our Values

Equal Opportunity Employer

Andersen Care is an Equal Opportunity Employer which means no candidate or applicant is treated less favourably as a result of their age, gender, sexual orientation, cultural and religious values and beliefs, race, creed, disability, appearance or any other personal attributes.

We value diversity and welcome applicants from all backgrounds. We work in collaboration with our clients to ensure strict adherence to all the relevant laws and regulations relating to equality and diversity.
