24-Hour Support

Live-in Carers

Remain in your own home with 24 hour live-in support.

Our live in Care packages could suit you if you need someone to be with you at all times of the day and/or night. In these cases one or two members of our team would live in your home and care for you, 24 hours everyday. We have Live-in-care staff in many counties of the UK and if our care staffs are not available in your area, we will travel to where you are.

As the staff we provide will be living in your home, we take extra care to properly match our staff with your situation, personality and clinical needs if any. All Our staff undergo Criminal Records Bureau checks and they all register with the Independent Safeguarding Authority (ISA). We do thorough pre-employment checks to give you peace of mind. The care you receive is of the highest standard.

Consideration for your home: All our staff are well informed about your preferences and expectations with regard to them living in your home. Our staff will bring their own personal items with them and treat your home with respect and consideration throughout their stay.

At Your Doorstep

Home Care Services

Professional care at Home. Our carers provide nursing, personal care and home help services around London and the Southeast.

The term 'Home care' covers a variety of different forms of care, and the number of names for it can be confusing – residential care, domiciliary care, live-in care, home care and community care being some of the variations. Some examples of our most commonly requested home care services are listed below. Please feel free to contact us on 0208 493 8742 if you have any questions or would like further information.

Personal Care

Carers assist with a wide range of personal care needs:

  • Bathing and showering
  • Shaving and grooming
  • Dressing and undressing
  • Incontinent care
  • Teeth brushing
  • Light housekeeping
  • Meal preparation
  • Errands and shopping
  • Medication assistance
  • Help with going to bed and out of bed
  • Changing linens, washing and grooming

Domestic Help (Home Help)

Andersen Care is more than willing to take on Home Help activities:

  • Vacuum cleaning
  • Laundry and ironing
  • Light domestic tasks
  • Washing dishes, dusting
  • Cleaning of toilets and bathrooms
  • Shopping, paying bills
  • Collection of pensions
  • Managing personal affairs (birthdays, anniversaries)
  • Monthly thorough house clean-up by specialist staff

Day Care

Carers are available to come throughout the day at specific times that you require the help or even stay for the entire day if this is necessary. Flexible scheduling to match your routine and needs.

Overnight Care / SleepIn

Carers come in the evening and settle you to bed, then stay over and sleep so that you have the comfort of knowing someone is there to help you during the night if the need arises. In some circumstances, carers are requested to stay the night and remain awake. If you feel that you need this service then we can help you.

Live-in Care

We can arrange a carer to live in with you. With our live-in care service, your care will follow a specific care plan tailored to your needs throughout the day or night. Continuous support with a familiar face.

Companionship and Sitting Service

Sometimes people would just like some company and to have someone to talk to. Or sometimes, if you are caring for a relative, you need to be able to go out confident they will be safe and happy while you are gone. Our home care companions can come for any length of time to play games, read the paper, go for a walk with you or just for a chat and to make a cup of tea.

Mental Health Care

At Andersen Care, it's all about retaining your comfort, personal dignity, independence and choice. We provide an appropriate care package to enable individuals with short, medium and long-term mental health conditions to lead an independent, happier and rewarding life at home and within their community.

Our care staff have received in-depth training in managing mental health issues, on the Mental Capacity Act, Managing behaviours seen as challenging and the safeguarding of vulnerable adults.

The support our care staff provide has been adapted to meet the specific mental health needs of every service user and is focused on the recovery model, emphasising each individual's genuine capability for progress and growth.

Disability Support

Physical or Sensory Impairment

At Andersen Care, we believe people with disabilities have the right to live as independently as they wish and in the way they want, whilst receiving care at home. We offer a specialist service for those individuals over the age of 18 who have a physical impairment.

Our specialist service is outcome focussed and person centred. We work with you, your families and friends to develop a person-centred plan that sets out your hopes, aspirations and dreams, and the choices you would like to make.

Having a disability, whatever your circumstances, is no longer a barrier to living a fulfilled and independent choice-filled lifestyle. Our flexible Homecare package is geared to ensuring you maintain your independence and lifestyle in a manner that respects your privacy and dignity.

Specialist Dementia Support

Dementia Care

Andersen Care ensures the coordinated delivery of care services to people with Dementia, thereby Promoting your Independence, Dignity, Privacy and Respect at all times.

Our model of service delivery is outcome focussed and person centred enabling us to be responsive to the needs of the individual service user. We believe that it is essential that staff providing support to people with Dementia are of a high calibre and adaptable to an ever changing environment.

Individuals with dementia are not treated as 'dementia sufferers' but as people who happen to have dementia and therefore, first and foremost, treated and valued as a person whose needs may differ.

Our outcome focussed, flexible approach means that we can scale the support we provide up or down by offering additional services like rehabilitative support, administration of medicines, managing challenging behaviour.

End of Life Support

Palliative Care

Rehabilitation Care

Support for your independent lifestyle.

At Andersen Care, we understand that everyone is an individual with different circumstances. When returning home after either an illness, accident or from hospital, you will require different levels of support during a varying recuperation period, to either regain or learn new living skills to maintain increasing independence and standard of living.

Our model of rehabilitation service delivery is outcome focussed and person centred, enabling you to rebuild your confidence, whilst achieving personal recovery and empowerment.

We work very closely with you, your families and other health professionals and external specialists to ensure you remain focussed on recovery.
