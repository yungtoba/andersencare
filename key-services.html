Funding Options

Funding Our Care Support Services

Quite often, people worry about how to fund the home care support and personal assistance that Andersen Care provides. That's why we will work with you to find the options that best suit you, your circumstances and your personal finances.

Some of our clients qualify for full or part-funding of their care through their local council, NHS trust or other bodies and these organizations can commission and pay for some or all the services we provide.

Alternatively, if you do qualify for funding you can control the funding yourself through a direct payment. With a direct payment, people who have been assessed as needing help by their local council's social services team can choose to arrange and pay for their own home care and support services instead of receiving these directly from the council.

You can also pay for our service privately. We'll design your support package so that it meets your needs and personal budget, set up a direct debit and send you regular statements of account so that you can see you are spending your money wisely.

Council / NHS Funding

Full or part-funding through local council, NHS trust or other commissioning bodies.

Direct Payments

Control your funding yourself and choose to arrange and pay for your own home care and support services.

Private Payment

Pay privately with direct debit setup and regular statements of account provided.
