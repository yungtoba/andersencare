Our People

Training for Care Staff

High calibre care assistants trained to the very highest standards.

Our Standards

High Calibre Care Assistants

Our Health Care Assistants come from varied ethnic backgrounds and ages. All must have care experience either working as a care assistant, a nurse, or in some cases having gained sufficient experience in health and social care from other care organisations.

All Andersen Health Care Staff are trained to very high standards and must undergo Mandatory training both when they first join us and regularly thereafter before given assignments.

This enables them to perform the required daily tasks involved in either running a household, personal care, Domiciliary care, Live-in care, while being responsible care workers and companions.

What We Cover

Comprehensive Training Programme

This package of training and development means that our staff are knowledgeable, experienced and comfortable in meeting the varying needs of our service users sensitively and professionally.

Household Management

Training in running a household efficiently, including domestic tasks, meal preparation, and maintaining a safe living environment for service users.

Personal Care

Comprehensive training in bathing, dressing, grooming, incontinence care, and maintaining dignity while assisting with intimate care needs.

Domiciliary Care

Specialised training for providing care in private homes, understanding home environments, and respecting personal space and preferences.

Live-in Care

Training for 24-hour residential care, including boundary management, privacy respect, and creating comfortable living arrangements.

Responsible Care Working

Development of professional responsibility, accountability, ethical decision-making, and compassionate communication with service users.

Companionship Skills

Training in social engagement, active listening, emotional support, and building trusting relationships with service users.

Compliance

Thorough Vetting Process

In line with the National Care Standards Act, we carry out checks with the Criminal Record Bureau and ensure that they are legally able to work within the UK before starting work with us.

All Nurses and Care Assistants undergo a thorough application process. As a result, we guarantee all staff are fully compliant and ready to work without delay.

  • Criminal Record Bureau (CRB) / Disclorure Barring Service (DBS) checks
  • Independent Safeguarding Authority (ISA) registration
  • Legal right to work in the UK verification
  • Thorough application and interview process
  • Reference checks from previous employers
  • Health & Social Care qualifications verified
