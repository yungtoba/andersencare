Welcome

Welcome to Andersen Care

Andersen Care is a leading provider of Homecare Services, Domiciliary Care and Supported Living Services that was founded after careful analysis of the spiralling demands on health and social care personnel. We recognised that the demand for quality temporary workers in the health and social care sectors often outweighs supply.

Working to the principles of fairness, equality, transparency and valuing people; Andersen Care successfully works in close partnership with Care Homes, Local authorities, Residential and Nursing Homes as well as Public and Private Sector, providing care services that promote independence and enable choice and control to individuals in their own homes.

The above services may be delivered on a one-one basis or through a team of professionals with the shared aim of achieving better service outcomes for the service user.

Our Expertise

Comprehensive Care Services

The range of services provided by the organisation include but are not limited to the following specialist areas.

Care for the Elderly

Specialist care for older adults promoting independence, dignity and quality of life in familiar surroundings.

Supported Living

Providing supported living services and accommodation for vulnerable adults within local communities and London Boroughs.

Mental Illness

Therapeutic support and care for individuals with mental illness promoting recovery, wellbeing and social inclusion.

Rehabilitation Care

Supporting recovery and re-enablement to help individuals regain independence and daily living skills.

Special Needs

Dedicated care for individuals with special needs ensuring personalised attention and daily living support.

Paraplegia

Comprehensive care for individuals with paraplegia focusing on mobility, independence and quality of life.

Learning Disabilities

Personalised support focusing on skill development, social inclusion and community participation.

Palliative Care

Providing comfort, dignity, and compassionate support for individuals with serious illnesses.

Eating Disorders

Specialised care and support for individuals managing eating disorders with sensitivity and understanding.

General Nursing & Care

Professional nursing and care services delivered with compassion, competence and clinical excellence.

Our Promise

Respect and Dignity

At Andersen Care, we place high significance on staff training and Service User protection. Service Users are treated with respect and dignity. Their needs are individually assessed and planned in liaison with the family (if they wish).

Our Care Staff are trained, competent and specifically selected based on the needs of the Service User. We believe that all recipients of our services should get best value and continuity of care as far as practicable.

We are committed to multi-agency working and will not spare any time or money to get our services to any agreed and expected standards.
